Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2025

Helping Hands for Hoarders (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides a mobile app designed to support individuals with hoarding tendencies. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

What We Collect

  • Name, email address, and profile photo (via Firebase or Facebook login)
  • User-created goals, preferences, and progress
  • App usage data to improve your experience

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your experience in the app
  • To save your progress and manage your avatar and goals
  • To provide secure authentication and sync across devices

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. All data is securely stored using Google Firebase services and used only for app functionality.
